In-class Programming: Blue Bikes

DS 4200, Northeastern University

In this assignment you will learn how to make a simple spatial visualization using a scatterplot with a map background.

Blue Bike rentals in March, 2017

Bluebikes is a bike share company that rents bikes for short-term trips in the Boston area. They have made various data publicly available. On this page, we are visualizing the locations of their bike stations in March, 2017.
